Our tracking technology

The GPS tracker is exactly the collar that will help you track your pet's location, almost anywhere in the world. Works with iOS and Android. Suitable for cats and dogs. Staya is a product of our experience and customer wishes.

This is the only 3G tracker on the market! All analogs work only in 2G networks. Our tracker works in 2G and 3G. In practice, this greatly affects the battery consumption. The tracker has an improved GPS antenna, its accuracy is up to 20 meters. If you use Petsee outdoors, the detection accuracy is up to 3 meters. You can swim and dive in our GPS collar, it is waterproof.

FAQ

  1. 01

    Selection and adjustment of the collar

    To get the most accurate size, measure up to the dog's neck. If the resulting girth is between the values in the table, it is better to choose the larger size.

  2. 02

    How to adjust the collar

    The collar will fit comfortably if no more than two fingers pass between the band and the withers for medium and large breeds and one finger for small breeds.

  3. 03

    How often to renew your subscription

    The subscription is renewed once a year.

  4. 04

    How to adjust the length

    Adjusting the length of the collar is simple: open the clasp and pull the band at a 90° angle.

  5. 05

    Is it possible to choose a cellular operator

    When buying, you can choose any cellular operator.

  6. 06

    What to do if a dog is missing

    An SOS button is available in our app, our help will help you.

  7. 07

    Is the track safe for the pet

    Our track is completely safe for your pet.

  8. 08

    Is the track suitable for cats

    Our track has a small size, it is lightweight so it is suitable for cats
